Ace Commodity

Traders face a perennial choice of attempting to pick a particular tip from amongst the many available free as well as paid stock tips. Keeping this dilemma in mind we at www.devangvisaria.com have devised Ace CommodityTM. This is a product which ensures that you not only trade selective right calls (RightCallTM) but get charged only for recommendations that have generated a certain set hurdle rate of return.

In simple terms we as the service provider will earn Money only if we make money for you! In addition to this we have a refund option wherein you can ask for a Refund in case you are not satisfied with the product. We will refund you 80% of the balance remaining subscription amount in a period of 7 working days no questions asked!

In Ace Commodities we offer the Trader an opportunity to selct either the Bullion Pack or the Base Metals pack or the Crude Pack.

    1. Book Full Profits and exit the position.
    2. In case of higher exposure than our suggested exposure we recommend to take part profits and hold the balance with a trailing at cost stop loss. We keep giving the updated levels and Follow ups based on the market conditions.
  • Traders are advised to take minimum exposure of Rs. 10 Lakhs as that is the base for our calculations of the profit of Rs. 9,000. They are free to take a higher exposure but as always we recommend trading on all calls at an equal exposure value.
  • We don’t keep more than 3 LIVE trading recommendations at any given point in time.
  • No charges in case the RIGHTCALL fails to hit the Rs. 9,000 profit hurdle rate.
  • A Client can ask for a refund on his balance subscription amount in case he is not satisfied with the services and he will receive back 80% of the balance amount.
